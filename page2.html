"My life for Aiur!"

Zealots are lower-ranked Templar who have yet to reach the upper levels of the
Khala. They form the backbone of the protoss military, and have been utilized
since before the Discord. At least some, if not all Khalai zealots are members of
the Zealot Order. Zealots honor the moon Saalok as their order's symbol, which
they call the Zealous Round. Zealots can operate in platoons and legions. During the
Great War, many zealots demonstrated their fearlessness by going without head
armor, a practice that has since been discontinued.
