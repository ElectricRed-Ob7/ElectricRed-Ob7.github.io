Zerglings form the most numerous of the zerg mutations. Their simple DNA
(distilled from the dune runners' genetic code to its most simple form) allows two
zerglings to be spawned from a single larva. The larvae get the genetic information
from the 'primordial ooze' of spawning pools. Zergling replication is so efficient that
they thrive even when exposed to exceedingly high mortality rates, which are
greater than almost all other zerg strains. While their core DNA comes from the
dune runner, zerglings are hybridized from over fifteen other organism strains.
